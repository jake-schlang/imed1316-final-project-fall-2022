Volunteer Opportunities

If you have a nack for photography and absolutely love hiking out in nature, this is the perfect volunteer opportunity for you! Join our Woodsman team and get experience in photography, land navigation skills, survival techniques, and have a fun time doing it!

What is the Woodsman team? The Woodsman team is M&P's premiere group of like-minded invidivuals who love the outdoors and all it has to offer. Not only will you enhance your skills as an outdoorsman, but develop livelong relationships that few people get to experience.

We encourage all to apply! Our team is crompised of veterans, wildland fire-fighters, survival experts, photographers, climbers and even some folks who are just getting started.

Custom Work

Need some custom work done? We can build maps and compasses from the ground up. You can either utilize our preset builder within each category or send us an email with your request. We will be more than happy to get you exactly what you need!

Our Maps and Compasses are made in house right here in the United States. We get our material from recycled parts and even have contracted forgers and craftsmen to get us the parts we need. You can even request that we do something hand made without machinery!

Some custom work pieces may take longer than others, but after submission we will give you a dedicated timeline for your product and keep you informed on how your item is doing at every step of the way. Click below to get started!
